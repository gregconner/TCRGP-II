   The header contains the most visible PII, yet it was not addressed.

   overlooked document-level metadata.

   There appears to be no established protocol or checklist for what
   needs to be de-identified. Without a systematic approach, it's easy
   to miss critical elements.

5. MANUAL PROCESS LIMITATIONS
   Manual de-identification is error-prone and time-intensive. It's
   difficult for humans to consistently identify and replace all
