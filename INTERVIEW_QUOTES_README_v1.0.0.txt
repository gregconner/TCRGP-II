METHODOLOGY
================================================================================

Quote Selection Process:
1. Each interview was analyzed for content addressing each survey question
2. Up to 4 most relevant excerpts were selected per interview per question
3. Quotes were elided (shortened) to focus on key content
4. Ellipses (...) indicate removed text
5. Character positions recorded for verification

Quality Standards:
- All quotes are verbatim from interview subjects
- Character positions allow verification against original transcripts
- No quote exceeds 3 sentences of elided content
- Quotes selected for relevance and clarity
- Coverage ensures all 9 questions addressed by all 4 interviews
